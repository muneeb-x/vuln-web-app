Security Vulnerability Lab

Sign-up isn't available yet

New accounts need email verification, and this copy of the app doesn't have an email (SendGrid) sender configured yet.

To enable sign-up, an operator must set SENDGRID_API_KEY and SENDGRID_FROM (a SendGrid-verified sender address) in the environment — locally in .env (copied from .env.example), or as environment variables on the host (e.g. Render). The full step-by-step is in the README's "Email Verification — Setup" section. After setting the values, restart the app.
